    Research interest

    I'm interested in global evolutionary biology; the stories behind the history of life and the methodologies with which it is investigated. I'm particularly attracted to ways of combining classic methods such as descriptive palaeontology with modern bioinformatic tools such as phylogenomics and mathematical modeling.

    I also try to actively communicate my work through teaching and promoting evolutionary sciences to general public (namely through the EcoEvo@TCD blog).
